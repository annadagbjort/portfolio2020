Rebranding a product

< go back

I redesigned the Icelandic chocolate milk, Kókómjólk. I chose that product because both their packaging and their message ("you get power from Kókómjólk") is quite outdated in my opinion. I decided to go out of my comfort zone in this project and wrote the documentation in Danish (since I am currently taking an elective in danish). I really enjoyed doing this project and am excited for doing more brand design projects in the future.

Duration

3 weeks

Group Size

I worked alone
